About Me

Welcome to my website. Let me tell you a little about myself: I am an IT professional, primarily as a project manager. I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Telecommunications Management. I have my PMP (Project Management Professional) certification and CSM (Certified Scrum Master) certification. Technology is constantly changing which is a big reason I got into this career area: I enjoy learning new skills and using the analytical side of my brain. I have many years of experience at Fortune 500 companies, such as Wells Fargo, Unitedhealth Group/Optum, Target Corporation, and Thomson Reuters.

Personally, I enjoy golfing, biking, walking, traveling, TV binge watching; and spending time with friends and neighbors during backyard summer outings just grillin' and chillin'. I grew up on a farm in Kansas where I learned what hard work was all about (I'm sure this is why child-labor laws exist today (lol)). I then moved to Minnesota in 1990 were I started my career as a Telecommunications Engineer.
